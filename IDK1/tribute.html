World War II

The grave-maker of generations

Here's a timeline of World War Two (1939-1945) :

  • 1st September 1939 - Hitler Invaded Poland
  • 3rd September 1939 - Britain and France declare war on Germany
  • May 1940 - Hitler invaded Denmark,Norway,Holland and Belgium
  • 26th May 1940 - British forces pushed back to the beach at Dunkirk
  • 22nd June 1940 - France Surrendered
  • 10th July - 31st October 1940 - Battle of Britain
  • 22nd September 1940 - Tripartite pact allied Germany,Italy and Japan
  • December 1940 - Italians Defeated North Africa
  • 22nd June 1941 - Hitler Invaded Russia-Operation Barbarossa
  • 7th December 1941 - Japanese attacked Pearl Harbor
  • 8th December 1941 - Britain and US declared war on Japan
  • 4th - 6th June 1942 - Battle of Midway
  • 23rd October - 11th November 1942 - Battle of El Alamein
  • 17th July 1942 - 2nd February 1943 - Battle of Stalingrad
  • 12th May 1943 - Axis forces surrendered North Africa
  • July 1943 - Allies Invaded Sicily
  • 3rd September 1943 - Italy Surrendered
  • November 1943 - Allies held Tehran Conference
  • 6th June 1944 - Operation Overlord - D-Day
  • 25th August 1944 - Paris Liberated
  • December 1944 - Battle of the Bulge
  • 28th April 1945 - Mussolini captured and executed
  • 30th April 1945 - Hitler Committed Suicide
  • 4th May 1945 - Germany Surrendered
  • 6th + 9th August 1945 - Atomic bombs dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki
  • 14th August 1945 - Japan Surrendered
